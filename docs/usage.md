# Usage guide

## How to use

1. Run `npm run dev` and open the printed URL.
2. Fill in your details in the left-hand form. The resume preview on the right updates instantly.
3. Use the **+ Add** buttons to add multiple experiences, education entries, projects, or certifications. Use **Remove** to delete one.
4. Your data saves automatically (see the status indicator in the top bar).

## Updating your resume later

Just reopen the app (`npm run dev`) in the **same browser**. Your previously
entered details load automatically. Edit anything, then export a new PDF.

> Note: data is stored per-browser on this computer. It is not synced across devices or browsers.
> If you clear your browser data or use a different browser, the saved resume won't be there.

## Start over

Click **Clear saved data** in the top bar and confirm. This clears your resume
content and resets the form to empty.
